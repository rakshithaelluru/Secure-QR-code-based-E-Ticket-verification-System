# Glossary Development

A glossary is created to define important terms used in the project for better understanding.

- QR Code: A machine-readable code used to store ticket information.

- E-Ticket: A digital ticket generated by the system.

- Admin: The person who manages and controls the system.

- Ticket Verifier: The person who scans and checks tickets at the entry point.

- Customer: The user who holds the e-ticket.

- Database: Central storage where ticket and user details are saved.

- Validation: The process of checking whether a ticket is valid or not.

- Scanner: A device or camera used to read QR codes.

- Report: A summary of ticket usage and verification details.
