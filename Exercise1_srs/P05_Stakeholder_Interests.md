# Stakeholder Interests



- Admin:
The admin wants to control and manage the system properly. They want to create users, monitor ticket verification, and keep the system running smoothly. The admin has high influence on the system.

- Ticket Verifier:
The verifier wants an easy and fast way to scan QR codes and check tickets. They want to reduce manual work and avoid mistakes. The verifier has medium influence on the system.

- Customer:
The customer wants quick entry without waiting in long lines. They want the ticket verification to be simple. The customer has low influence on the system.

- Event Organizers:
They want to stop fake and duplicate tickets and manage crowd entry properly. They have high influence on the system.
