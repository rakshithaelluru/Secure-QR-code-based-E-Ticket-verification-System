# SRS Introduction
# Purpose:
The purpose of this Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document is to describe the functional and non-functional requirements of the Secure QR Codeâ€“Based E-Ticket Verification System. # Scope:
The scope of the system includes generation of digital tickets with unique QR codes, storage of ticket details in a centralized database, and real-time verification of tickets at entry points. The system is designed to prevent ticket fraud and reduce manual verification.

# Definitions and Acronyms:

QR Code: Quick Response Code used to store ticket information.

Admin: Person who manages and controls the system.

Ticket Verifier: Person who scans and validates QR codes at entry points.

E-Ticket: Electronic ticket generated by the system.

Database: Central storage of ticket and user information.
