# Stakeholder Analysis

Stakeholders are people who are involved in or affected by the system.

# Primary Stakeholders (Directly use the system):

Admin – Manages and controls the system.

Ticket Verifier – Scans QR codes and checks ticket validity.

Customer – Uses the QR code ticket to enter.

# Secondary Stakeholders (Indirectly involved):

Event Organizers – Use the system to reduce ticket fraud.

Transport / Theatre Management – Benefit from faster verification.

Supervisors / Management – Use reports for monitoring.
