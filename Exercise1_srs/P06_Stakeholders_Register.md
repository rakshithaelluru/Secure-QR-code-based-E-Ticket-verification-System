# Stakeholder Register
| Stakeholder                        | Role                 | Responsibility                                                       | Interest                               | Influence |
| ---------------------------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | --------- |
| **Admin**                          | System Administrator | Manages users, controls the system, and monitors ticket verification | Proper system functioning and security | High      |
| **Ticket Verifier**                | Entry Staff          | Scans QR codes and verifies tickets                                  | Fast and easy ticket checking          | Medium    |
| **Customer**                       | End User             | Uses QR code ticket for entry                                        | Quick and smooth entry                 | Low       |
| **Event Organizers**               | Service Provider     | Organizes events and manages ticket usage                            | Prevent ticket fraud and manage crowd  | High      |
| **Transport / Theatre Management** | Management Authority | Oversees system usage and operations                                 | Secure and efficient verification      | High      |
